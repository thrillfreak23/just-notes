# ovid book one note
[[ovid-1.md]]
Line 24. Both Deus ("quisquis deum..." whoever of the gods it was...) and
melior natura (nature meaning natural processes; melior meaning better than the
chaos that came before) are what assembled the world. Thus the Stoics who
demanded a Demiurge were satisfied, and the Epicureans too, who said it was
Nature. Ovid had it both ways.
